Hi, I'm Molly. I'm a licensed therapist in Atlanta, and I specialize in the kinds of conversations that feel too complicated — or too vulnerable — to have anywhere else.

Whether you're untangling your sense of self, figuring out who you are in relationships, exploring your identity, or just trying to feel less anxious about being alive — you don't have to do it alone.

My Approach

"Therapy works best when it feels like a real conversation — not a performance."

I draw from relational, psychodynamic, and humanistic frameworks — which is a fancy way of saying: I pay attention to patterns, I care about your history, and I believe you already hold most of the answers. My job is to help you find them.
